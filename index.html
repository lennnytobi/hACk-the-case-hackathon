FAQ

Answers to common questions

What is "hACk the case"?
▼
"hACk the case" is a full-day hackathon for students interested in tech and/or consulting. You can learn, connect, and win prizes alongside like-minded students.
Who can participate?
▼
The event is open to students from Munich with diverse backgrounds. You don't need to know how to code; you just need to be curious and genuinely interested in the field.
Do I need a team?
▼
No, you don't need a team prior to the event. You can apply as a team or find one at the event. We will help form teams at the beginning.
What should I bring?
▼
Bring your laptop and charger. We provide workspace, Wi-Fi, and snacks.
How do I register?
▼
You can register through Luma using the "Register Now" buttons or directly via this link.
How much does it cost?
▼
The event is completely free for participants. We provide the space, food, and beverages for the event.
What's in it for me?
▼
You get the opportunity to learn and connect with like-minded students from Munich and with industry professionals who will support you during the event. You can also win great prizes!
